================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. Kinematic MPC + High-Gain PD ≈ Complex Dynamics MPC
   Simple approach with fast feedback beats sophisticated modeling

2. Adaptive Gain Scheduling > Trajectory Shaping
   Time-varying PD gains most effective for startup optimization

3. MPC Frequency > Prediction Accuracy
   40 Hz update rate more important than long horizon

4. IK + Feedforward > Dynamics MPC (for dual-arm)
   When IK is accurate, simple feedforward control suffices

5. Dynamics MPC Still Challenging
   Model errors and integrator mismatch persist
